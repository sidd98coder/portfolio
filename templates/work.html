001 : Password-Checker

Introduction

A data breach is an incident that exposes confidential or protected information. A data breach might involve the loss or theft of your Social Security number, bank account or credit card numbers, personal health information, passwords or email.
Data breaches are rampant and many people don't appreciate the scale or frequency with which they occur. By aggregating the data here I hope that it not only helps victims learn of compromises of their accounts, but also highlights the severity of the risks of online attacks on today's internet.
"Have I been pwned" is a website developed by Troy Hunt who is an Australian web security expert. This site contains over 600 millions of breached passwords and emails. These passwords or emails are used by black hat Hackers in a loop for accessing sites. It's better to check the status or security of your passwords regularly.

How it works ?

This program can tells you whether your password had been ever hacked or not. It also gives information on number of times your password has been used by someone else.
Based on the concepts of APIs and data scraping using Python, This program can helps user to know about the security of their passwords. Just give a password that you want to check and it will show how many times it was used to hack your account.
Skills Used : Python3, Knowledge of APIs, Data Manipulation(data scraping), Analytical skills
